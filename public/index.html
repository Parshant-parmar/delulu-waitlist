What is Delulu?

Delulu is a college-only app built for the courage to talk to someone new — classmates, hallmates, people in your clubs, people you'd never work up the nerve to approach in the dining hall. You connect through real conversation, not a photo grid, and reveal faces only when you're both ready. Where it goes from there — friendship, connection, or something more — is completely up to you. Verified campus by campus, so it's always someone real, from your own college.

Not everyone needs a dating app. Everyone needs the courage to talk to someone new. Delulu is built to take the fear out of that first move.

FAQ

Is this a dating app?

Delulu is built around real conversation first — most people use it to build the confidence to talk to someone new, make friends, and find their people on campus. If a connection turns into something more, that's completely your call. Nothing is forced or assumed.

Is Delulu live yet?

Not yet — we're in the pre-launch waitlist phase. Joining now reserves your place for early access.

Which colleges are supported?

We're starting with a small set of partner campuses and expanding at launch. Only official college email domains are accepted.

Will joining the waitlist create my account?

It reserves your spot and verifies your college email now, so sign-up is instant the moment we launch.

Is my information safe?

Yes. Your information is securely stored, chats are end-to-end encrypted, and your personal data is never shared with other users.
